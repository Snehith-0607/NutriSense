## 🧠 Why NutriSense?

People don’t fail diets because they lack knowledge.  
They fail because they don’t know what to eat **in the moment**.

NutriSense acts as a **real-time decision assistant**, helping users:
- Understand what they eat  
- Improve meals instantly  
- Make smarter food choices throughout the day  

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🍽️ AI Meal Logger
- Log meals using text (e.g. *"2 rotis with dal"*) or image  
- AI analyzes and estimates:
  - Calories  
  - Protein, carbs, fat  
- Generates:
  - **Health Score (0–100)**  
  - Insight + improvement suggestion  

---

### 📊 Daily Dashboard
- Calories consumed vs goal  
- Macronutrient breakdown  
- Dynamic health score  
- Meal timeline  
- AI-powered daily insights  

---

### 🔥 Smart Recommendation Engine
NutriSense doesn’t just analyze food — it actively suggests what to eat next based on:
- Current intake  
- Nutritional gaps  
- User goal  

Example:
> “You’re low on protein → Try paneer bhurji or boiled eggs”

---

### 💬 AI Nutrition Coach
- Chat-based assistant  
- Context-aware responses using:
  - User goal  
  - Meal history  

Example queries:
- “What should I eat next?”  
- “Is my diet balanced today?”  

---

### 🎯 Goal-Based Personalization
- Weight Loss  
- Muscle Gain  
- Balanced Diet  

Automatically adjusts:
- Calorie targets  
- Nutritional recommendations  

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. User logs a meal (text/image)  
2. AI analyzes food and returns structured nutrition data  
3. Health score is calculated  
4. Insights and suggestions are generated  
5. Dashboard updates in real-time
